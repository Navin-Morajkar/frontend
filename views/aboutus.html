Delta@STAAR is an ERP software made for DELTIN company considering their requirements, which stressed on making administrative work simple and easier. The software provides a solution for managing DELTIN'S Goa's all accomodations from one place and through one portal. The software consists of different modules which deal with water supply, rooms and complaints. Our team has worked tirelessly to deliver this project and has tried to ensure that the portal is user friendly.
